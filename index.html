Being a Treatise on Wisdom, Balance, and Proper Timing

In the tradition of scholars who seek truth through observation of the natural world

This grimoire presents a system of natural philosophy grounded in empirical observation and the wisdom of three guiding principles: Thoth, who provides the accumulated knowledge of those who came before; Ma'at, who ensures ethical balance and natural order; and Caerus, who reveals through observation when conditions align for successful work.
